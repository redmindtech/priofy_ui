4.Safe Operating & Limits
SAFE OPERATING & LIMITS
  • Steam drum level should be maintained at 50 % (low-level total trip sp is 18%, partial trip sp is 40% and low alarm sp is 42%)
  • Furnace draft (firebox pressure) should be maintained < -4 mm H20 column (low draft total trip sp is 0 mm H2O, partial trip sp is -2 mmH2O).
  • Fuel gas system pressure must be maintained > than 0.056 kg/cm2g and < 2.8 kg/cm2g to avoid furnace trip.
  • Ensure that the furnace area is cleared of all personnel before initial automatic light off.
