HAZARDS & PRECAUTION

HAZARDS
PRECAUTION

Hot piping/equipment and potential risk of burning if flames exit sight ports and furnace doors during draft upset conditions.

System contains hot condensate, BFW & steam which can cause severe burn.
Proper protective clothing must be worn. Full sleeve and leather gloves must be used.

Nitrogen asphyxiation

Nitrogen is a known asphyxiant, be away during depressurizing, barricade the area and put warning signs of N2 asphyxiation

Flammable Area

FRC.
use gas meter

Noise

Single hearing protection Ear Plug or Ear Muff.

Sand Storm

Comfort mask.

Heat Stress

Follow Heat stress guideline published in CLIENT PAS (Public Address System)
Use cooling vest
Drink plenty of water
During Peak Summer (June till September) perform this task during 6 PM-6 AM.

Ergonomic issues like strain and sprain

Use neutral body posture while opening valves, lubricate valves, take breaks.

Pinch/Hit hazards

Use leather gloves, ensure all sharp edges are identified and covered.
