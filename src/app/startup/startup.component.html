3.Before You Begin
BEFORE YOU BEGIN
Confirm that the "Furnace, Prepare to Startup" procedure has been executed.
Verify that the "Process Fluid Introduction Checklist" (PFIC) has been completed if the furnace underwent prolonged shutdown or significant maintenance. If unsure, consult the technical staff for guidance.
Close and cap or plug all open-ended vents and drain valves on the Ethane/DMS/DMDS/Fuel/Cracked Gas lines to prevent any leaks or spills.
Ensure that all drains on the Primary, Secondary, and Tertiary TLEs (Thermal Liquid Exchangers) are securely closed to maintain system integrity.
Confirm that both the steam drum and secondary TLE blowdown valves are closed to maintain pressure and prevent any unintended release of fluid. These preliminary checks are essential to ensure the safe and smooth startup of the furnace operation.
