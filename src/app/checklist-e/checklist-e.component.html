10.Procedure Checklist-E
Procedure Checklist #E—Warm Up COT
Step Action (Hazard/Precaution if applicable) Yes/No
1 OOT to ensure that all lower level burners are lit before starting light off of upper level burners.
2 IOT to continuously monitor the fuel gas pressure and OOT to continue lighting off more burners as required
3 OOT to open desuperheater Untreated BFW manual block valves (DB&B) when the COT is > 450 ºC.
4 IOT to confirm that the desuperheater valve does not pass. If it passes, instruct the OOT to close the B/V until desuperheating flow is needed.
5 When steam drum pressure is above 90 kg/cm2g:
*OOT to line up steam drum continuous blowdown valve and block in steam drum intermittent blowdown.
*OOT to confirm that the sTLE blowdown to CBD header is lined up.
*OOT to ensure steam drum blowdown analyzer online and confirm cooling water flow to and from cooler.
*OOT to ensure sTLE blowdown analyzer cooling water flow to and from cooler is established.
*OOT to open desuperheater untreated BFW manual block valves (DB&B) if not done before.
*IOT to enable steam drum and sTLE continuous blow downs from HMI Station.
6 If steam PSV EVT testing needs to be done, hold COTs at 700°C (via COT manual set point) and steam drum pressure at 93 kg/cm2g using steam vent to atmosphere control valve (flow should be around 12 M t/h).
*Continue with warm-up when PSV EVT testing is complete
7 OOT&IOT to reconfirm that Decoke Air is blinded (ONIS device).
8 When COT temperature is above 760ºC and if steam flow confirmed higher than 10 Mt/h per side, it is possible to move the Furnace sequence to Swing MOV step, if ready to do so.
